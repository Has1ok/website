phētēs

Group exhibition at Garage Studios, 2022

Sound installation with dandelion seeds and air currents

As children, we loved to catch fluff seeds or thistledown, we all knew that catching one meant receiving a letter from afar. If you managed to catch the fluff, then it was addressed to you. Now you could whisper a response and release the "letter". Plant seeds are carriers of messages from the past to the future. In installation secret messages, carried by dandelion seed, become audible.

The work was created with the support of the Garage Studios and Artist Residencies of the Garage Museum, Moscow.
