we have nothing to do with it

Video and computer graphics: Hasmik Badoyan
Music: Arash Azadi
Performance: Hasmik Tangyan

2019, a contemporary dance and poetry performance "Excitement and Call" is about to begin inside a modernist fountain at the entrance to a metro station in the center of Yerevan, when it gets interrupted by a conservative group, who accuses artists of Satanism and LGBT propaganda. Film follows the unraveling of events, floating between protesters and performers, catching candid moments and short-lived stories, while the difference between protest and performance becomes more and more vague.
